## Chronological Timeline

| # | Date | Headline | Description |
|---|------|----------|-------------|
| 1 | Nov 29, 1962 | **Born in Fort Wayne, Indiana** | Son of John J. "Jack" Byrne, who would become CEO of GEICO and chairman of White Mountains Insurance Group. Grew up in Woodstock, VT and Hanover, NH. |
| 2 | c. 1975 | **First meets Warren Buffett** | Through his father's insurance career, young Patrick meets Buffett. His parents allowed him to skip school to absorb lessons on business and investing. Byrne later calls Buffett his "Rabbi" and lifelong mentor. |
| 3 | 1984 | **Contracts Hepatitis C in China** | While in Xinjiang, China, a barefoot doctor sews up a head wound under unsanitary conditions, resulting in Hepatitis C infection — a condition that would shadow him for decades. |
| 4 | 1985 | **Graduates from Dartmouth College** | Earns BA with dual majors in Western Philosophy and Asian Studies. Also studied at Beijing Normal University, earning a certificate. |
| 5 | 1985 | **Diagnosed with testicular cancer** | Collapses during a vacation in Scotland weeks after graduation. Diagnosed with seminoma that had metastasized to his lungs. Begins three separate bouts over roughly three years; hospitalized nearly three years total, then three more years of convalescence. |
| 6 | c. 1985-1987 | **Studies in China** | Studies at Beijing Normal University. Spends extended time in China and Thailand — deepening exposure to Eastern philosophy and languages. |
| 7 | 1987-1989 | **Marshall Scholar at Cambridge** | Studies philosophy at King's College, Cambridge University on a Marshall Scholarship. Earns Master of Arts degree. |
| 8 | 1989-1991 | **Teaching fellow at Stanford** | Serves as teaching fellow at Stanford University while simultaneously managing two private companies: Blackhawk Investment Co. and Elissar, Inc. |
| 9 | 1994-1997 | **CEO of Centricut, LLC** | Runs industrial plasma torch manufacturer in Concord, NH. Steps in after the prior CEO's abrupt departure. |
| 10 | 1996 | **Earns PhD in Philosophy from Stanford** | Dissertation covers intellectual origins of the U.S. Constitution, with emphasis on mathematical logic, moral philosophy, and economics. |
| 11 | 1997-1998 | **CEO of Fechheimer Brothers (Berkshire Hathaway)** | Runs uniform manufacturer for police, fire, and military. Recruited directly by Warren Buffett to lead the Berkshire subsidiary. |
| 12 | Spring 1999 | **Invests in D2: Discounts Direct** | Puts $7 million for a 60% equity stake in a struggling online liquidation startup. |
| 13 | Sept 1999 | **Takes over as CEO of D2** | Assumes day-to-day leadership of the company, beginning a 20-year run as chief executive. |
| 14 | Oct 1999 | **Company renamed Overstock.com** | Rebrands from D2: Discounts Direct to Overstock.com, positioning it as an online closeout retailer. |
| 15 | 2000 | **Bicycles cross-country for cancer research** | Rides from San Francisco to the East Coast to raise funds and awareness for the Dana-Farber Cancer Institute. Offers $1 million of his own money if donors match $11 million. |
| 16 | May 2002 | **Overstock.com IPO** | First major U.S. company to use the Dutch auction method for its IPO. Sells 3 million shares at $13/share, raising approximately $39 million. |
| 17 | 2005 | **Launches anti-naked-short-selling crusade** | Files suit against hedge fund Rocker Partners alleging illegal naked short selling of Overstock stock. Hosts "The Miscreants' Ball" webcast, referencing a mysterious "Sith Lord" (later identified as Steve Cohen). |
| 18 | 2005 | **Founds Deep Capture** | Launches deepcapture.com, a personally funded investigative media outlet created to bypass what Byrne calls "captured" institutions mediating national discourse on Wall Street corruption. |
| 19 | 2006 | **Named chairman of Friedman Foundation** | Becomes chairman of the Milton & Rose Friedman Foundation for Educational Choice (now EdChoice) following Milton Friedman's death. |
| 20 | Feb 2007 | **Files $3.5 billion lawsuit against Wall Street firms** | Overstock and Byrne sue 11 prime brokerages including Goldman Sachs and Merrill Lynch, alleging deliberate market manipulation through phantom share sales. |
| 21 | 2007 | **Major school voucher campaign** | Funds over $4 million of $5 million raised by Families for Choice PAC supporting Utah's school voucher referendum. |
| 22 | c. 2005-2015 | **Builds schools across developing world** | Founds 22 private schools in Africa, India, Asia, and Latin America — named for his mother Dorothy Byrne. Collectively educate over 7,000 students. |
| 23 | Nov 2011 | **Ernst & Young Entrepreneur of the Year** | Named National Entrepreneur of the Year 2011 (Retail & Consumer Products) at the Strategic Growth Forum in Palm Springs, CA. Recognized for innovation in online retail and philanthropy. |
| 24 | Jan 9, 2014 | **Overstock becomes first major retailer to accept Bitcoin** | First major U.S. retailer (then $1.4B in annual revenue) to accept Bitcoin. Receives over 800 orders worth $126,000 on day one; first purchase is a $2,700 patio set. |
| 25 | 2014 | **Founds Medici Ventures** | Creates wholly owned Overstock subsidiary focused on leveraging blockchain technology for securities trading, property rights, and financial infrastructure. |
| 26 | 2015 | **First digital bond purchase on Bitcoin blockchain** | Uses Medici's tZERO platform to execute the world's first purchase of a digital bond entirely on the Bitcoin blockchain. |
| 27 | July 2015 | **Meets Maria Butina** | Encounters Butina at a libertarian conference. Contacts the FBI, beginning a years-long role as a government informant. Their relationship spans roughly three years. |
| 28 | Jan 2016 | **Overstock settles naked short selling litigation** | Merrill Lynch settles for $20 million. Multiple other defendants including Gradient and Copper River also settle, ending the decade-long legal battle. SEC bans naked shorting in the process. |
| 29 | Apr 2016 | **Takes leave of absence for Hepatitis C treatment** | Announces indefinite leave from Overstock to address complications from the Hepatitis C he contracted in China in 1984. Completes treatment and returns. |
| 30 | Dec 2016 | **First blockchain-based public stock offering** | tZERO executes the world's first SEC-compliant, FINRA-approved alternative trading system (ATS) blockchain security — a milestone in financial technology history. |
| 31 | Oct 2017 | **Announces $250M tZERO ICO** | At Money 20/20 conference, announces a dramatic pivot from retail toward blockchain with a $250 million initial coin offering for tZERO. |
| 32 | July-Aug 2019 | **Goes public about FBI informant role** | Reveals to media that the FBI directed him to pursue a romantic relationship with Maria Butina and re-establish contact ahead of the 2016 election. Former FBI Director Comey and other officials deny the claims. |
| 33 | Aug 22, 2019 | **Resigns as CEO of Overstock** | Steps down as CEO and from the Overstock board, citing "deep state" machinations. Jonathan E. Johnson III appointed as successor. Overstock shares had fallen roughly 40% following his disclosures. |
| 34 | Sept 2019 | **Divests Overstock equity** | Sells nearly all remaining equity stake in the company he founded, completing his separation from Overstock after 20 years. |
| 35 | Nov-Dec 2020 | **Enters 2020 election fray** | Studies evidence of alleged election fraud. Approaches attorney Sidney Powell. Begins financing and organizing efforts to challenge the results. |
| 36 | Dec 18, 2020 | **Attends unannounced Oval Office meeting** | Meets with President Trump, Sidney Powell, and Gen. Michael Flynn. Argues the election was stolen; urges Trump to seize voting machines via executive order. |
| 37 | Feb 2021 | **Publishes "How Trump Lost the White House" blog series** | Multi-part firsthand account of the post-election efforts, detailing meetings at the White House and interactions with Trump's inner circle. |
| 38 | Apr 2021 | **Co-founds The America Project** | Launches nonprofit with Gen. Michael Flynn and Joe Flynn, focused on election integrity and constitutional rights. Byrne personally funds roughly $27 million of approximately $30 million in total expenditures through 2023. |
| 39 | 2021 | **Publishes "The Deep Rig"** | Self-publishes *The Deep Rig: How Election Fraud Cost Donald J. Trump the White House, By a Man Who Did Not Vote for Him* (ISBN: 9780578865935). Largely compiled from his blog posts. |
| 40 | June 26, 2021 | **Premieres "The Deep Rig" documentary** | Film directed by Roger R. Richards debuts at Dream City Church, Phoenix, during the Arizona Senate audit of Maricopa County. Reported budget of $750,000. |
| 41 | Feb 2022 | **Launches election integrity initiative at CPAC** | Alongside Gen. Flynn and Roger Stone, unveils bi-partisan election monitoring program. The America Project organizes "Operation Eagles Wings" across eight states. |
| 42 | July 15, 2022 | **Testifies before Jan. 6 House Select Committee** | Nearly 8-hour closed-door session. Transcript released December 30, 2022. |
| 43 | Nov 2023 | **Hunter Biden files defamation lawsuit** | Biden sues Byrne in U.S. District Court for the Central District of California over allegations Byrne made on The Alex Jones Show and in a Capitol Times Magazine interview regarding a purported Biden-Iran bribery scheme. |
| 44 | Feb 13, 2024 | **Publishes "Danger Close"** | *Danger Close: Domestic Extremist #1 Comes Clean* released by Defiance Press (ISBN: 9781963102109). Full memoir covering FBI informant years, Maria Butina, Russia investigation, and 2020 election. Preface by Butina; prologue by Gen. Flynn. |
| 45 | Sept 2024 | **Relocates to Dubai** | Moves to the UAE citing a $25 million bounty allegedly placed by the Venezuelan government in July 2024, as relayed to him by a DEA agent. Also claims threats from criminals in West Africa. Lawyers notify U.S. courts he has left the country "for the foreseeable future." |
| 46 | Oct 2025 | **Found in default in Hunter Biden case** | California judge finds Byrne in default for failing to defend himself and raises possibility of $5 million in punitive damages. Byrne's attorneys move to set aside the default, calling him a "whistleblower" with "tapes" that can prove his claims. Jury trial pushed to January 2026. |

## Philosophy & Intellectual Life

- PhD dissertation at Stanford focused on the intellectual origins of the U.S. Constitution, mathematical logic, moral philosophy, and economics
- Marshall Scholar at King's College, Cambridge
- Dual major at Dartmouth in Western Philosophy and Asian Studies
- Studied at Beijing Normal University
- Lifelong interest in both Eastern and Western philosophy; spent time studying in China and Thailand
- Often references philosophical frameworks in business decisions and public commentary
